1 I Too Had a Love Story Ravinder Singh "I Too Had a Love Story" by Ravinder Singh is a heartfelt tale of love and loss. Ravin, the protagonist, meets Khushi through a matrimonial website. Their relationship blossoms through calls and messages, and they fall deeply in love, planning to get engaged. However, tragedy strikes when Khushi meets with a fatal accident just days before their engagement. The story beautifully captures the joy of true love and the pain of losing it, leaving readers with a lasting emotional impact.
2 Can Love Happen Twice? "Can Love Happen Twice?" by Ravinder Singh follows Ravin as he tries to move on after losing Khushi. In Belgium, he meets Simar and falls in love again. However, differences arise when they return to India, leading to heartbreak. The story explores whether one can truly love again after a great loss.
3 Wings of Fire Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam. "Wings of Fire" is the autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, tracing his journey from a small town in Tamil Nadu to becoming India’s “Missile Man” and President. It highlights his struggles, achievements in space and defense projects, and inspires readers to dream big and work hard.
4 Girl in Room 105 Chetan Bhagat "The Girl in Room 105" by Chetan Bhagat is a suspenseful story about Keshav, who finds his ex-girlfriend Zara dead in her hostel room. Determined to uncover the truth, he embarks on a journey to solve the mystery behind her murder.
