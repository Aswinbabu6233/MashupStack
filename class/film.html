Kilukkam

Kilukkam (1991) is a timeless Malayalam comedy-drama directed by Priyadarshan, featuring Mohanlal, Revathi, and Jagathy Sreekumar in lead roles. The story follows Joji, a carefree tourist guide in Ooty, who unexpectedly meets Nandini, a quirky and mysterious young woman claiming to be an orphan in search of her father. Along with his photographer friend Nischal, Joji reluctantly takes her in, leading to a series of hilarious and heartwarming events. As the plot unfolds, Nandini's seemingly innocent story reveals a deeper mystery, bringing both danger and drama into their lives. The film is a perfect blend of humor, emotion, and suspense, elevated by stellar performances, especially the comic brilliance of Jagathy and the chemistry between Mohanlal and Revathi. With its iconic music and engaging narrative, Kilukkam remains a classic that continues to entertain audiences across generations.
