LPG Price Stability in Kerala: November 2024 Overview

  • Overview
  • Domestic LPG Prices
  • Consumption Trends
  • Inflation Context
  • Conclusion

In November 2024, Kerala's liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) market demonstrated a period of relative stability for domestic consumers, with prices for 14.2 kg LPG cylinders remaining unchanged across major cities. This consistency brought relief to households amidst rising inflationary pressures and volatile global energy markets. Domestic pricing stability is attributed to effective government interventions, including subsidies aimed at cushioning the impact of international price fluctuations and ensuring affordability for consumers. On the other hand, commercial LPG rates showed a marginal upward adjustment, reflecting broader trends observed across India due to increasing transportation costs and fluctuating crude oil prices. The rise in commercial rates highlights the ongoing challenges faced by small businesses and industries that rely heavily on LPG, even as efforts to promote cleaner energy solutions remain a priority in the state.

Consumption Trends

Nationally, the consumption of petroleum products, including LPG, grew by 2.9% in October 2024 compared to the same period the previous year.While specific data for Kerala in November is pending, the state is expected to mirror this national growth trend, given its consistent LPG usage patterns.

Inflation Context

The year-on-year inflation rate based on the All India Consumer Price Index (CPI) for November 2024 stood at 5.48%. The stability in domestic LPG prices in Kerala during this period suggests effective management of household energy costs amidst broader inflationary pressures.

Conclusion

November 2024 was characterized by stable domestic LPG prices across Kerala, providing relief to households amid rising inflation. However, the increase in commercial LPG prices indicates potential cost pressures for businesses reliant on LPG. Monitoring these trends will be crucial for stakeholders to navigate the evolving energy market landscape.
